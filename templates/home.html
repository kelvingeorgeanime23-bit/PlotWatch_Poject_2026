Life On The Plot

What to expect day to day

Water day hasn't changed

Every Tuesday, one tap, bring your jerricans. A 20 litre jerrican costs KSh 10. PlotWatch keeps track of who's paid.

Garbage, sorted

Every house gets a rubbish sack. Collection runs once a week, every Wednesday. Fill it, tie it, leave it out.

Gate locks at 11pm

Every tenant gets their own gate key. Coming home late never means waking the plot.
